What I do

✉

Email Marketing

I build email programs from the ground up or work with what's already there. Strategy, copy, design, deployment — whatever the job needs. I've worked mainly in Brevo and Zoho Campaigns. I can pick up other platforms quickly but those are where I've spent most of my time.

⚡

Automation

Welcome sequences, re-engagement flows, cart recovery, post-purchase follow-up. Set up right the first time, these run without needing anyone to babysit them.

🌐

Web Updates & Builds

Landing pages, campaign pages, ongoing site maintenance. I write clean HTML and CSS and can work in most CMSes. I turn things around fast and don't need hand-holding on requirements.

📊

Copy & Content

Email copy, web copy, blog posts. I try to write in the client's voice, which means I ask questions first and write second. Takes longer but the output is actually usable.

🎯

Campaign Planning

Before anything gets built, I want to know who we're talking to and what we actually want them to do. The strategy comes first. Everything else — when it goes out, where, what it says — follows from that.

📈

Reporting

I send a weekly note covering what went out, what the numbers looked like, and what I'm working on. I skip the vanity metrics. If something's underperforming I'll flag it directly rather than burying it in a chart.
